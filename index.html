Project Name: WorldSurf

Project Description

As technology increases so does the quality of websites, which in turn is creating a more demanding workplace for web designers.

Long gone are the days of a solid background a few links and lots and lots and... lots of filler words to make up for usability and interaction.

This site will be a comprehensive account of the next four weeks of Bonnie Carter's Web Design Class and the interface design feed through Karma Points on the Usability Hub

Dafney the Miniature Schnauzer has maxed out her Karma Points and is exhausted. We all need some vitamin D. Don't forget to put the mouse down and go outside every once in awhile.
